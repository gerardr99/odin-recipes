Macaroni

Description

This dish features al dente macaroni pasta tossed in a rich tomato sauce with savory sausage slices. It's a simple and hearty meal, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Leek
  • Red pepper
  • Chilli
  • Rehydrated dried tomato
  • Natural chopped tomato
  • fresh chorizo
  • White wine
  • Laurel leaf
  • dried oregano to taste
  • Sugar
  • Ground black pepper
  • Cheese to gratin to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Shall

Steps

  1. We begin by cooking the macaroni in plenty of salted water following the instructions on the package, trying to leave it al dente. I like to mix the smooth variety with the grated variety, but it is totally optional.
  2. Drain well and reserve in a bowl or on a tray.
  3. Chop the onion, garlic cloves, leek, pepper and seeded chilli.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200ºC.
  5. Heat a little oil in a frying pan or casserole and sauté the onion with the garlic a little.
  6. Add the rest of the vegetables and cook over medium heat until they color.
  7. Add the chopped dried tomato, the drained canned tomato, the bay leaf and a little salt and sugar.
  8. Stir well and cook over medium heat for about 10 minutes.
  9. Add a splash of wine, let the alcohol evaporate and add oregano.
  10. Cook for 10-15 more minutes. Blend the sauce, removing the bay leaf and set aside.
  11. Chop the chorizo, removing the skin or cut it into not very large pieces and brown it in the same pan.
  12. Add the tomato sauce and macaroni and cook for a couple of minutes.
  13. Transfer to a baking dish, add the cheese and gratin until browned to taste.
  14. Serve with freshly ground black pepper and a little fresh basil, if we have it.
